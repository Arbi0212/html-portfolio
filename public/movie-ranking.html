The Best Pakistani Dramas According To Arbiya

My top 3 Pakistani Dramas of all-time.

Ranjha Ranja Kardi

It's a drama full of emotions, I love the acting of Imran Ashraf.

Meri Zindagi Hai tu

The beautiful love story drama, I love yearners that's why I love this Drama.

Baaghi

I cried a lot while watching this drama. A innocent girl got killed by her brother, it's a biographical drama based on the life of Qandeel Baloch.
